§ 01 — Welcome

A note from the UNFILTERED team.

Why we're doing this, what we believe, and what to expect from the first edition.

We started this because the Baltics deserve a serious room for low-intervention wine — one that respects the craft, the people, and the truth in the glass.

Anton Zhilin · Festival Curator · LOWINE

Thank you for making the journey to Rīga for the very first UNFILTERED Natural Wine Festival. We are incredibly grateful you are here.

Our goal is to establish this as an annual milestone that elevates and expands the low-intervention, natural wine movement across the Baltics. Honestly, we are obsessed with the level of craftsmanship represented by this year's participants — and we want your experience here to be entirely seamless.

This page is your operating manual: where to be, when to be there, where to eat, who to call. Save it, share it, and tell us afterwards what we got wrong.

Cheers to an incredible weekend, — The UNFILTERED Team
