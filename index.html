01
A note from the UNFILTERED team

Welcome to Rīga.

Thank you for making the journey to Rīga for the very first UNFILTERED Natural Wine Festival. We are incredibly grateful you are here.

Our goal is to establish this as an annual milestone that elevates and expands the low-intervention, natural wine movement across the Baltics. Honestly, we are obsessed with the level of craftsmanship represented by this year's participants, and we want to ensure your experience here is entirely seamless.

We have designed this guide to help you navigate our city and your schedule effortlessly. Your comfort is our priority, and we welcome your candid feedback after the event so we can refine and improve everything for next year.

Cheers to an incredible weekend,

— The UNFILTERED Team

Agenda · Saturday 30.05

Festival day. VEF Kvartāls.

The main event. Below is your full day — from arrival and prep through public doors.

12:00

Arrival & Preparation

We suggest arriving at 12:00 — plenty of unhurried time to set up your table, chill bottles, and prep your space. Specialty coffee waiting for you, brewed by Rocket Bean Roastery.

SPITTOONS · ICE · FRESH WATER — ALL PROVIDED ON-SITE
13:00 ↓ 16:00

HoReCa Trade Session · B2B

A dedicated window reserved exclusively for hospitality professionals, importers, sommeliers and trade buyers. Let your business grow with us.

Trade list on request · glouglou@unfiltered.lv
16:00 ↓ 18:00

Winemaker Intermission · Free time

A two-hour break between B2B and public sessions. This time is entirely yours — relax, eat, mingle with fellow winemakers, and recharge before the evening crowd arrives.

18:00 ↓ 22:00

Public Tasting · Doors open

The main event. Showcase your wines, your craft and your story to an eager, wine-loving public. Smaller pours, longer conversations, real curiosity.
